Past Events

Countryside Medical Club has created a garden at Mease Countryside Hospital to help brighten the days of both staff and patients, providing a peaceful space that adds beauty and comfort to their environment.

Numerous guest speakers have visited to share valuable information about their careers, offering students insight into different medical and health-related professions.

Countryside High Medical Club made handmade flowers for patients at Mease Countryside Hospital, taking time to create something cheerful and heartfelt to brighten their day and show support for the local community.

The Safety Harbor Fire Department visited the Countryside High Medical Club, taking time to speak with students, share insights about their work, and answer any questions they had about emergency medicine and public safety.
